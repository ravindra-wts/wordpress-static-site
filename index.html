Equitas Debit Card Launch

Together with Equitas Bank, we hosted a virtual launch event of a debit card which catered to more than 50000 people from all over the world and is considered to be one of the largest Metaverse events. The event consisted of stunning virtual features for the participants like having a stroll in the mimicked cricket stadium and pavilion. In the centre of the cricket ground, resided a rotating debit card which was the crux of the event.

Our Workflow

Phase 01

Mutual Ideation

Determining what the Metaverse will be used for, who the target audience is, and what features and functionality are necessary to meet the project's objectives.

Phase 02

Moving From Concept To Creation

Crafting a comprehensive plan with a layout and navigation design for the virtual environment. Building the infrastructure and databases. Developing engaging user activities through the efforts of our highly creative team.

Phase 03

Enriching the Metaverse

Here, the focus shifts towards enriching the Metaverse with captivating content that aligns with the project's objectives and engages the target audience. Content creation plays a crucial role in bringing the virtual environment to life and providing users with immersive experiences.

Phase 04

Precision in Motion

Thoroughly testing the newly created Metaverse to ensure a seamless and bug-free experience. Presenting a thoroughly tested, engaging environment while continually striving for improvement and enhancement.
